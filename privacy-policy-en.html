🔒 Privacy Policy

For: 🎵 Sleepzee - Sleep & Meditation

📌 Our Core Promise: Sleepzee does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data. All data is stored locally on your device only.

📱 1. What Data We Collect

Short answer: Nothing.

Sleepzee is a completely offline application that does not require an internet connection to use any features. Therefore:

  • ❌ We do not collect your name, email, or phone number
  • ❌ We do not collect your location information
  • ❌ We do not collect your usage habits or device information
  • ❌ We do not use any third-party analytics or advertising SDKs

💾 2. How Data Is Stored

All content you create in Sleepzee (such as custom scenes, favorite sounds) is stored only locally on your device:

  • 📱 Stored in local storage on your iOS device
  • 🔐 Other apps cannot access this data
  • 🗑️ All data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app

🌐 3. Does Data Transfer to Servers

No. Sleepzee has no servers; all features run on your device:

  • ✅ No account registration required
  • ✅ No login required
  • ✅ Can be used without internet connection
  • ✅ Data is not uploaded to any cloud

👶 4. Children's Privacy

Sleepzee is suitable for users of all ages, including children under 13:

  • 👍 We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13
  • 👍 In fact, we do not collect any user data at all
  • 👍 Children can safely use all features

🔐 5. Third-Party Services

Sleepzee does not integrate any third-party services:

  • ❌ No advertising networks
  • ❌ No analytics SDKs (such as Firebase)
  • ❌ No social media SDKs
  • ❌ No payment processing (except through Apple App Store)

📲 6. Device Permissions

Sleepzee only requests necessary permissions:

  • 🔊 Media & Apple Music: For background audio playback (continues when screen is off)
  • 📱 Local Network: For AirPlay casting to speakers (optional)

These permissions are only used to provide core functionality and are not used to collect data.

🔄 7. Privacy Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time:

  • 📅 Updated policy will be published on the App Store page
  • 📧 Major changes will be notified via in-app notification
  • ✅ Continuing to use the app indicates your acceptance of the updated policy

🇪🇺 8. GDPR Compliance (EU Users)

If you are located in the European Union, under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the following rights:

  • 📋 Right of access: Since we do not collect data, no access is needed
  • 🗑️ Right to erasure: Uninstall the app to delete all local data
  • 📤 Right to data portability: Not applicable (no data stored on servers)
  • ❌ Right to object: Not applicable (no data processing)

🇨🇦 9. CCPA Compliance (California Users)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • 🚫 We have not sold any consumer's personal information in the past 12 months
  • 🚫 We have not collected any personal information
